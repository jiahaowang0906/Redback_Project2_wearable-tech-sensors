How to Use Our Product

A simple guide for elderly users

Step 1: Unboxing

Open the package carefully and remove the product. Ensure all components are included.

Step 2: Charging

Charge the device using the provided charger. Wait until the battery is fully charged before use.

Step 3: Power On

Press and hold the power button to turn on the device. Wait for the startup screen to appear.

Step 4: Navigation

Use the large buttons or touch screen to navigate through menus. Follow on-screen instructions.

Step 5: Adjust Settings

Access settings to customize font size, volume, and other preferences for a comfortable experience.

Step 6: Troubleshooting

If you encounter any issues, refer to the user manual or contact our customer support for assistance.
